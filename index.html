A support system for building support systems.

Whether you're looking to collaborate on a project, or you have a project of your own, we're a support system providing accountability, solidarity, and mentorship.

We meet once a week for heads-down work or group collaboration time, and once a week for pitches, brainstorming, progress reports, and retrospectives.

Sustainable systems for a sustainable economy.

Our economy can't count on growth ad infinitum.

From revenue to source code, everything we build is transparent and auditable—whether it's new software or ethical, sustainable alternatives to popular software.

Software should serve users. It shouldn't serve users on a silver platter.

We're not out to make a profit. We're out to revolutionize a new ethos of what software utilities could look like for the people that use it.
